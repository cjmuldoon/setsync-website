Features
Virtual Clips
Slice individual reps and sets from long session videos — no duplicate files. Mark start and end, or let auto-detection find your lifts.
Skeleton
AI pose estimation tracks 19 body joints through every phase. Automatic knee, hip, elbow, and shoulder angle measurement.
Bar Path
Tap the bar plate. Watch the trajectory trace through your lift. Compare paths between attempts, sessions, or athletes.
Compare
Side-by-side, overlay, or ghost outline. Up to 4 lifts simultaneously with synced playback. Save comparisons to revisit later.
Form Check
AI-powered form feedback against 44 movement templates. View-angle-aware analysis — different checks for side vs front camera.
Forge
Build highlight reels. Stitch clips on a timeline, add skeleton overlays, text, watermark. Export for Instagram, TikTok, or your coach.
Coach Programs
Coaches build structured training programs with sets, reps, and movement targets. Athletes load programs and submit videos against each prescribed lift.
Coach Portal
Full web dashboard for coaches. Review athlete submissions with annotations, track progress over time, manage your entire roster remotely.
Progress
Track joint angles and form scores over time. See trends across sessions. Quantify improvement instead of guessing.
Capture
Record at up to 240fps slow motion directly in the app. Step through frame by frame. See what real-time misses.
