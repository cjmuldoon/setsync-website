2. Description of Service

SetSync is a video analysis application designed for strength athletes and coaches. It allows users to record training videos at 120fps or 240fps, import videos from their Photos library, and analyse movement using on-device pose estimation (19 joints) and bar path tracking. The app provides side-by-side and overlay video comparison, annotations, angle measurements, and rep counting. All video processing and analysis is performed on your device using Apple's Vision framework.
